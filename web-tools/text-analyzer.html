This text analyzer retrieves the number of characters, letters, words, sentences, paragraphs, and lines! It also gives estimates of the reading time and the reading level of your text (such as 5th grade, University level, etc.). In addition to all that, it returns two frequency/density lists. One list for the frequency of each character and the other list for the frequency of each word. Both lists can be downloaded as separate CSV files! It's simple, fast, intuitive, and completely ad-free!
