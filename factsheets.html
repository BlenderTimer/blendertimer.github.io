Factsheets

Our Solar System:

Planet Factsheet Data such as diameter, density, volume, gravity, orbital parameters, and more for Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Dwarf Planet Factsheet Data such as diameter, magnetic field, orbital parameters, and more for Ceres, Pluto, Haumea, Makemake, and Eris.

Other Data:

Bible Data Data about the KJV Bible such as the center verse, the number of letters, the longest words, and much more.

The primary purpose of these factsheets is to provide data in one place to make research easier and quicker (and because data is fun)! The data for the space factsheets was mostly collected from NASA and the IAU to hopefully provide the most accurate measurements.
