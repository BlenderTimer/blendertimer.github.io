BlenderTimer

Free resources for EVERYONE!

BlenderTimer is the work of Daniel Roberts, a self-taught photographer, videographer, digital artist, YouTube creator, and developer. In an age of money-hungry corporations, Daniel Roberts has set out on a mission to provide the world with thousands of completely free resources, no caveats! This website is the home base of the entire business. Here, you can explore unique web tools like the Morse Code Translator and Size Comparison Tool, or dive into fun activities such as The World Since You. Watch engaging, educational videos exploring science, history, and other fascinating topics! Delve into the visualizations that challenge perception and illustrate space and time in remarkable ways! Browse an ever-expanding library of completely free stock media, including photos, videos, music, sound effects, illustrations, GIFs, vector graphics, and 3D models! With extensive factsheets, useful software, and a welcoming Discord community, BlenderTimer brings science, data, creativity, and fun together in one place! Built and maintained by me, Daniel Roberts, founder and owner of BlenderTimer.

If my work has helped or inspired you in any way, please consider supporting me and the free resources I provide.

Thank you for visiting BlenderTimer, I hope you enjoy your stay!
